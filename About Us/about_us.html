Our Story


First Brunch of Golden House At 1995,Our Director,Lee Kiat Boon stared his first store at Kuala Lumpur with a small store that only sell a little type of books and stationery.Start of the buiness,Mr Lee only can got his book suplies from Johor that remoted from store.He need to drive for 3 hours every week.We can felt that Mr Lee's road as a buinessman.After his unremitting effortsï¼Œthe small book store has became in right way.Mr Lee had opened a second brunch at Pahang and soon Pinang in 2000 and 2002.Besides,he also surcessful to cooperate with some company and agency such as,Popular Book Store,National Library, school and public libaries.In 2005,Mr Lee made a significant decision,that was Mr Lee wanted to expand the market to other country and not only at domestic.However,this decision was combated by family members and other cooperate company as this was too risky on the time,but he believe himselff and take over it. He and his shop managers took a risk by got a huge loan from Maybank to open a brunch at Singapura.The Singapor Brunch was completed and opened.As the saying goes,Heaven will not disappoint the person who tries.He got his first million on his whole life. Those company that didn't Optimistic about him start to invest to his
store.Mr Lee also turned their operation mode from a single store to group company named,
Golden House Group Company.
